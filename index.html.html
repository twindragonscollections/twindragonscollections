Policies & Notices

↩

Returns & Exchanges

We do not accept returns and exchanges. If you receive an item not in the condition advertised, please send photos by email to mickey@twindragonscollections.com. All claims must be made within 5 days of receipt.

📦

In-Stock & Pre-Order Shipping

If one transaction includes a pre-order item, all items ship once the last pre-order item becomes available. To receive an in-stock item immediately, please make separate transactions.

✕

Cancellations

Any orders cancelled after 2 business days incur a 30% cancellation fee. Please reach out as soon as possible if you need to change your order.
